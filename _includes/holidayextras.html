From London

The HS1 fast train from St Pancras gets you to Ashford. From there you can jump on the next train to Westenhanger. Send us a message on the meetup.com page - we might be able to give you a lift from Ashford.

When you arrive at Holiday Extras

From the carpark, make your way to the main front entrance and follow the signs for codeHarbour. Look for people with pizza and beer. You've made it!
