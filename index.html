From London

The HS1 fast train gets you to Ashford. From there you can jump on the next train to Westenhanger. Send us a message on the meetup.com page - we might be able to give you a lift from Ashford.

When you arrive at HX

There’s a car park here. The door to the meetups is around the side - walk past the main front door, take a right at the end of the building and there will be a door on your right. Look for people with pizza and beer.
